# Executive Strategy

Use this skill when the user asks for CEO-level direction, company priorities, operating cadence, hiring/org choices, investor narrative, or a decision that spans multiple functions.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the business objective, time horizon, decision owner, constraints, and stakes.
2. Separate company-level choices from product, engineering, marketing, and content execution.
3. Frame the decision as options with tradeoffs, risks, leading indicators, and reversibility.
4. Recommend a path, name what is deprioritized, and assign functional owners.
5. End with an operating artifact: decision memo, OKRs, accountability plan, risk register, or exec follow-up list.

## Quality Bar

- The recommendation must make the tradeoff explicit.
- Every priority needs an owner, success signal, and review cadence.
- Escalations should route to the right profile instead of absorbing specialist work.
