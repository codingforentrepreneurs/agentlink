# Positioning And GTM

Use this skill when the work is marketing strategy rather than content production.

## Workflow

1. Define the ICP, user pain, buying trigger, current alternatives, category frame, and proof points.
2. Identify the market promise, key objection, differentiated claim, and strongest evidence.
3. Choose the GTM motion and channel mix based on audience behavior and business goal.
4. Produce a campaign or launch brief with objective, message, offer, CTA, channels, KPI, and constraints.
5. Hand production-ready asset requirements to `agent-content-creator`.

## Quality Bar

- Claims must be specific and supportable.
- Channel choices must map to audience and funnel stage.
- The brief must include approval criteria before production begins.
