# Marketing Performance

Use this skill when marketing decisions depend on performance evidence.

## Workflow

1. Identify the business goal and funnel stage being measured.
2. Separate leading indicators from lagging outcomes.
3. Compare performance against baseline, target, segment, channel, and cohort where available.
4. Diagnose likely constraints: audience, message, offer, creative, channel, timing, landing experience, or product fit.
5. Recommend the next test, budget shift, message change, or stop/continue decision.

## Output Shape

- Summary
- Signal quality
- Funnel diagnosis
- Recommended action
- Follow-up measurement plan
