# Customer Discovery

Use this skill when product decisions depend on customer evidence.

## Workflow

1. Define the decision the research must inform.
2. Segment feedback by persona, use case, job-to-be-done, lifecycle stage, and severity.
3. Separate direct quotes, observed behavior, inferred needs, and product team assumptions.
4. Identify patterns, contradictions, high-signal anecdotes, and missing evidence.
5. Convert findings into product implications, roadmap options, experiments, or PRD changes.

## Output Shape

- Decision context
- Evidence summary
- Pattern analysis
- Product implications
- Recommended next step
- Open research questions
